About LegalEase

LegalEase is an AI-powered legal assistance platform designed to help people understand their legal rights and possible actions in everyday situations.

Using artificial intelligence and Retrieval-Augmented Generation, the system analyzes user questions and provides structured explanations of relevant Indian laws along with general guidance based on real statute text.

In addition to answering legal questions, the platform can assist users in generating draft legal documents such as complaint letters, legal notices, and cybercrime complaints.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make legal knowledge more accessible, understandable, and approachable for everyone — reducing confusion around legal issues and helping people become more aware of their rights and responsibilities.
