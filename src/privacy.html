Privacy Policy

Effective date: 01 January 2026  ·  LegalEase India

This Privacy Policy explains how LegalEase ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects personal information when you use our Service.

1 What Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide Directly

  • User profile data: name, email, phone (if provided).
  • Questionnaire inputs and document content you enter (facts, incident descriptions, names, dates, uploaded files such as screenshots or evidence).
  • Account credentials (passwords stored securely / hashed).
  • Support requests and communications.

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, timestamps, logs, IP address, browser/OS, device identifiers.
  • Cookies and tracking technologies for analytics and session management.

c. Third-Party Data

If you connect third-party services (e.g., cloud LLM providers, identity providers), those services may share data with us in accordance with their own policies.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Service (generate documents, respond to queries).
  • Perform retrieval and citation of legal texts (RAG pipeline).
  • Enable downloads, previews, and exports (DOCX/PDF).
  • Personalize user experience and store drafts.
  • Maintain security, detect abuse, and perform analytics.
  • Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

3 Document Generation and Third-Party Processing

Your inputs and generated outputs may be processed by third-party AI providers we use for polishing or inference. These providers' use of data is subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

We recommend not submitting extremely sensitive personal data unless necessary. If you must, consider local or offline workflows.

4 Storage, Retention and Deletion

We store user inputs, generated drafts, and logs to provide the Service and to allow editing/retrieval. By default, we retain user-submitted content and generated documents for 180 days from last access unless otherwise required by law.

You can request deletion of your personal data and generated documents by contacting us. We will delete data within a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal or operational constraints (e.g., backups, audit logs, or records required for abuse investigations).

We may retain anonymized or aggregated usage data indefinitely for analytics and model improvement.

5 Sharing and Disclosures

We may share data:

  • With service providers who perform services for us (hosting, vector DB, cloud LLMs, analytics). These providers process data on our behalf.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • To enforce our Terms, protect rights and safety, or to investigate fraud or abuse.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users).

6 Security

We employ commercially reasonable technical, administrative, and physical safeguards (encryption in transit, secure servers, access controls) to protect data.

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. If a breach occurs, we will notify affected users and take remediation steps as required by law.

7 Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to provide the Service, remember preferences, and analyze usage. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that some features may not function if cookies are disabled.

8 Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13 (or higher minimum age as required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child under the applicable age has provided information, contact us to request deletion.

9 Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Export your data (data portability).
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

10 Data Transfers

We may transfer and store data on servers located in different countries. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers. If you are in jurisdictions with data protection laws (e.g., GDPR), we will use appropriate safeguards (standard contractual clauses or equivalent).

11 Automated Decisions and AI Disclosure

The Service uses automated processing (AI models) to analyze user inputs and generate documents. Automated outputs are assistive and should be reviewed by users.

AI can make errors or hallucinate. The Service provides citations to aid review. You must independently verify any legal claims before acting.

12 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. If changes are material, we will provide notice via the Service or email. Continued use after updates means you accept the revised policy.
